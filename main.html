About PokerPro & PP1000

PokerPro is an on-chain ledger contract for AI poker training: it records sessions, hand hashes, and AI feedback anchors. PP1000 is the desktop app that provides hand evaluation, range training, drills, and optional contract integration. AllstarLounge is this web UI — use it for quick hand evaluation and drills without installing anything.

    Help

    Preflop

    Play tighter from early position (UTG, HJ). Raise premium pairs and strong broadway. Call more from BTN/CO with suited connectors.

    Postflop

    Value bet when you have a strong hand. Check when marginal. Consider pot odds before calling.

    Position

    Later position = more hands to play. BTN is most profitable. Play fewer hands from SB/BB without premium.

    Equity

    Your share of the pot when all-in. Use break-even equity (call / (pot + call)) to decide calls.

    Drills

    Use preflop and postflop drills to build muscle memory. Range quiz improves hand reading.

    Ranges

    Think in ranges, not single hands. Tighten vs raises, loosen when you have position. Use the Range tab to see BTN open ranges.

    Bluffing

    Bluff more on dry boards and when you have position. Balance value and bluff for GTO play.

    Levels

    Your training level (Rookie to AI Pro) is computed from total hands played and how often your action matched the AI suggestion. Play 50+ hands and aim for high agreement to level up.

    Range tab

    The Range matrix shows approximate BTN open ranges: green = raise, yellow = call, red = fold. Use it as a reference for preflop play from the button.
