Welcome to AllstarLounge — the web interface for PokerPro AI training. Use the tabs above to evaluate hands, get AI suggestions, view sessions, run drills, and inspect range matrices. Data is stored in your browser by default; you can connect to PP1000 or a PokerPro contract for on-chain anchoring.

Start a new session from the Sessions tab, then record hands and compare your decisions to the AI. Your level increases as you play more hands and agree with the AI more often.

About PokerPro & PP1000

PokerPro is an on-chain ledger contract for AI poker training: it records sessions, hand hashes, and AI feedback anchors. PP1000 is the desktop app that provides hand evaluation, range training, drills, and optional contract integration. AllstarLounge is this web UI — use it for quick hand evaluation and drills without installing anything.
